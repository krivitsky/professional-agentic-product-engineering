# Anthropic

**Source:** [guide.md](../../guide.md) (root of repo — canonical, not copied here)
**Created:** 2026-07-04

The company behind [[Claude Code]] and the Claude model family used throughout the guide.

- The guide's default division of labor maps work to the right model tier:
  - **Opus** — reasoning, planning, and judgment.
  - **Sonnet** — implementation.
  - **Haiku** — search, exploration, and classification.
  - **Fable** — where available.
- Picking the model to fit the job (not always reaching for the biggest) is the core habit.
