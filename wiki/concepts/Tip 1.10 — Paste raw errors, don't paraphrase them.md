# Tip 1.10 — Paste raw errors, don't paraphrase them
**Part of:** [[Tier 1 — Professional Prompting]] · tip 10 of 14
**Source:** `guide.md #tip-1-10` (root — canonical, not copied)
**Created:** 2026-07-04

> **Instead of:** "It throws some null error."
>
> **Prefer:** paste the full stack trace → "diagnose the root cause before changing anything."

The stack trace carries the file, line, and call path a paraphrase drops. Hand over the raw text and ask for root cause before any edit.
