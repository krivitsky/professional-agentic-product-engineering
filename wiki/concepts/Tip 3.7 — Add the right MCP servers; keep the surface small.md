# Tip 3.7 — Add the right MCP servers; keep the surface small
**Part of:** [[Tier 3 — Context Management]] · tip 7 of 8
**Source:** `guide.md #tip-3-7` (root — canonical, not copied)
**Created:** 2026-07-04

> **Instead of:** copy-pasting data in and out by hand, or wiring 30 thin tools.
>
> **Prefer:** connect a few high-value MCP servers (browser, DB, issue tracker) and let Claude use them directly.

Few powerful gateway tools beat many thin REST mirrors — >~20k tokens of MCP definitions already eats your working context.
