# Tip 4.6 — Ask for all findings, not a conservative review
**Part of:** [[Tier 4 — Loop Until Done]] · tip 6 of 9
**Source:** `guide.md #tip-4-6` (root — canonical, not copied)
**Created:** 2026-07-04

> **Instead of:** "Only flag high-severity issues." (current models obey and hide real ones)
>
> **Prefer:** "List every issue with a severity label and line reference. Don't pre-filter — I'll triage."

Asking the model to pre-filter makes it hide real issues; ask for every finding with a severity label and do the triage yourself.
