# Tip 1.3 — Say what to do, not what to avoid
**Part of:** [[Tier 1 — Professional Prompting]] · tip 3 of 14
**Source:** `guide.md #tip-1-3` (root — canonical, not copied)
**Created:** 2026-07-04

> **Instead of:** "Don't be verbose."
>
> **Prefer:** "Write for senior engineers — lead with the technical specifics."

A prohibition says where not to go, not where to go — the agent still has to invent the target. A positive instruction names it.
