# Tip 4.8 — Run a pre-mortem; treat it like a teammate
**Part of:** [[Tier 4 — Loop Until Done]] · tip 8 of 9
**Source:** `guide.md #tip-4-8` (root — canonical, not copied)
**Created:** 2026-07-04

> **Instead of:** "Looks great, ship it."
>
> **Prefer:** "What could fail here? What did you assume? What's missing?"

Treat the agent like a teammate and ask it to surface failure modes, hidden assumptions, and gaps before you ship.
