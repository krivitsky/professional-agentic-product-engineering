# Tip 1.7 — Follow the house style, don't invent one
**Part of:** [[Tier 1 — Professional Prompting]] · tip 7 of 14
**Source:** `guide.md #tip-1-7` (root — canonical, not copied)
**Triad slot:** context
**Created:** 2026-07-04

> **Instead of:** "Write a date parser."
>
> **Prefer:** "Parse dates using the pattern already in @utils/date.ts."

Point at the existing pattern in the repo. Left to invent, the agent picks a reasonable style that doesn't match yours — now you own two conventions.
