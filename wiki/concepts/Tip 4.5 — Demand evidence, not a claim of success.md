# Tip 4.5 — Demand evidence, not a claim of success
**Part of:** [[Tier 4 — Loop Until Done]] · tip 5 of 9
**Source:** `guide.md #tip-4-5` (root — canonical, not copied)
**Created:** 2026-07-04

> **Instead of:** accepting "done, it works."
>
> **Prefer:** "Paste the exact test command and its output before you say it's done."

A claim of success is not proof — require the command and its output so "done" is something you can see, not take on trust.
