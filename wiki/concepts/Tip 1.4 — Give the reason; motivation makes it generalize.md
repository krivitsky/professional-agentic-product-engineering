# Tip 1.4 — Give the reason; motivation makes it generalize
**Part of:** [[Tier 1 — Professional Prompting]] · tip 4 of 14
**Source:** `guide.md #tip-1-4` (root — canonical, not copied)
**Triad slot:** context
**Created:** 2026-07-04

> **Instead of:** "Use tabs."
>
> **Prefer:** "Use tabs — our linter rejects spaces in this repo."

The "because" lets the agent apply the rule to cases you didn't list. A bare instruction covers only the case you stated.
