# Tip 2.6 — Turn big features into a spec, then a fresh session
**Part of:** [[Tier 2 — Shaping and Slicing]] · tip 6 of 8
**Source:** `guide.md #tip-2-6` (root — canonical, not copied)
**Created:** 2026-07-04

> **Instead of:** a 200-message thread that drifts.
>
> **Prefer:** "Interview me on the hard parts, write SPEC.md" → new session: "Implement SPEC.md."

Shape the behavior into a spec — a Story, Given/When/Then scenarios, and Non-goals — so "done" is concrete before any code, then implement it in a fresh session.
